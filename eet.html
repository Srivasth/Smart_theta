EMPLOYEE EFFICIENCY TRACKER

Employee Effiot will solve your employee management problems for the most part. You can rate your employees and based on your requirements, machine learning will be introduced to make your task of making employees accountable easier and more effective

CASE STUDY

The quality for a shaft(say) which the employee works on will be monitored, uploaded and linked with the employee list.

The quantity – for a given quality and time, number of shafts manufactured and the numbers will be linked to the employee list as well.

By algorithms, the employee’s efficiency will be constantly monitored and if fallen below a threshold, the supervisors will be notified. Thus, the job of supervising is made efficiently productive.
